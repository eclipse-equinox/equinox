[Equinox](http://www.eclipse.org/equinox/) is a sub-project of the
[RT](RT "wikilink") top-level project. Equinox was formerly a
sub-project of the [Eclipse](Eclipse "wikilink") top-level project. The
bulk of the Equinox related pages can be found in the **[Equinox Article
Index](:Category:Equinox "wikilink")**. There are however some pages
that serve as entry points into the different activities in Equinox.

## Schedule

The Equinox team harmonizes it schedule with the Eclipse release trains.
In addition to the release and milestone events, there are a number of
conference calls and meetings. All of this is tracked in the [Equinox
Calendar](http://www.google.com/calendar/embed?src=08i9q5g17o9il95b4pbc5n4f4g%40group.calendar.google.com&ctz=America/Toronto)

The calendar is available in the following formats:
[ICal](http://www.google.com/calendar/ical/08i9q5g17o9il95b4pbc5n4f4g%40group.calendar.google.com/public/basic.ics),
[ATOM News
Feed](http://www.google.com/calendar/feeds/08i9q5g17o9il95b4pbc5n4f4g%40group.calendar.google.com/public/basic),
[HTML](http://www.google.com/calendar/embed?src=08i9q5g17o9il95b4pbc5n4f4g%40group.calendar.google.com&ctz=America/Toronto)
